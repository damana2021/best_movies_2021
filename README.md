<h1 style="font-family:courier;"> Best movies of 2021</h1>
<h2 style="font-family:courier;"> Here is a list of movies that everybody loved in 2021</h2>
</p style="font-family:courier;"> 1. No Time To Die</p>
<img src="https://pics.filmaffinity.com/No_Time_to_Die-525355918-large.jpg" alt="No Time To Die (2021) - Filmaffinity" style="width:277.083px;height:441px;">
</p style="font-family:courier;"> This one is a pure classic in the world of action films. James Bond saga is beloved by millions of people all around the world. This edisode of Bond has literally everything we could dream about: brilliant actors, great cgi, breathtaking scences. Another reason to watch it is that No Time To Die is the fifth and the last movie featuring amazing Daniel Craig as J.B.</p>
</p style="font-family:courier;"> Soundtrack: "No Time To Die" by Billie Eilish</p>

</p style="font-family:courier;"> 2.Dune</p>
<img src="https://m.media-amazon.com/images/M/MV5BN2FjNmEyNWMtYzM0ZS00NjIyLTg5YzYtYThlMGVjNzE1OGViXkEyXkFqcGdeQXVyMTkxNjUyNQ@@._V1_FMjpg_UX1000_.jpg" alt="Dune (2021) - IMBb" style="width:277.141px;height:411px;">
</p style="font-family:courier;"> Actors, background, story - this movie will make history. Dune of 2021 is just a start of movie series based on another series of self-titled novels by Frank Herbert. And if you think, that movies are always worse, than their origins, Denis Villeneuve will prove you wrong.</p>

</p style="font-family:courier;"> 3.The Matrix Resurrections</p>
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/5/50/The_Matrix_Resurrections.jpg/220px-The_Matrix_Resurrections.jpg" alt="The Matrix Resurrections - Wikipedia" style="width:220px;height:327px;">
</p style="font-family:courier;"> We've heard a lot about horrible movie remakes, but this one cannot be called one of those. The reason for that is the director of Resurrections: Lana Wachowsky - the original movies' director. Since the release of the Matrix Trilogy, the story gained a huge fanbase and the universe we want to believe in opens again.</p>
